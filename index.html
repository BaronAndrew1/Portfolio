The Scenery

This was our two week capstone project at The Iron Yard. The project, pitched by a fellow student, was to create a website for live performance. It allows a standard user to find local live entertainment be it plays, street performances, or even local bands. The site relies on professionals to sign up, enter their information, and place their performances and showtimes, at which time they can be easily searched via the front page by genre, city, or state. It is still in a relatively unpolished form, and is still undergoing modifications. While I was a Front End Developer on this project, the application contains both a Ruby on Rails back end and an AngularJS front end.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, AngularJS
